Discover the Beauty of Tanay Rizal



A hidden gem in Rizal, Philippines, as you explore its breathtaking landscapes and indulge in exciting outdoor activities. Tanay's hiking trails, picturesque waterfalls, and serene swimming spots will provide you with unforgettable memories and a rejuvenating experience.

Cielo Alto Place

Escape the city and create new memories with your loved one at Cielo Alto Place, located in Sitio Mayagay, Tanay, Rizal. This newly-opened venue offers breathtaking mountain views and cozy accommodations perfect for an intimate celebration or a romantic getaway.

Treasure Mountain

Enjoy an outdoor dining experience and have a taste of their restaurant’s silog breakfast and Filipino meals You can choose to simply unwind in the area or venture on various activities such as their Obstacle Course with Spider's Web Activity, Bosay Falls Trek, Maximinus Pool Swim, The Apiary Farm Visit, Laiban Falls Trek, El Patio Razon Visit, or Daranak Fall Trek.

3 Spring Mountain Park

A Nature Park and Campsite in Tanay, Rizal with a panoramic view of the Sierra Madre Mountain Range. The Park offers open areas and facilities perfect for large-scale outdoor activities.
